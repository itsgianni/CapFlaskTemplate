Our Story

After asking many of my peers “If you could have one thing right now, what would it be?”, nearly all of them replied “money”. Following that question, I asked them: “If I gave you $10,000 right now, what would you do with it?”, and the majority said they would immediately spend it all. This has worried me because once these individuals grow up and start receiving wages, they will likely make unwise financial decisions and will live without financial freedom. Since money is essential for society to function, why is finance not a mandatory course taught in highschool? Even after looking through internet for courses on financial literacy, I find not one that teaches in an engaging, affordable and effective way. Therefore, I set out to create a website that educates users about the important skills of saving and investing, and I hope to spread this information to all that need it. This information is for anyone who is willing to learn. It doesn't matter if you have absolutely zero knowledge or if you are already an expert; everyone is welcome!
