What is budgetting?

Budgetting is essentially when you analyze the relationship between your income and expenses and use that information to make decisions about your money. If you are tight on money, you can prioritize your spending and focus your money on things that are most important to you.

Why is budgeting important?

You can stay out of debt by reducing the risks of overspending, it can relieve financial stress, and it can help you save money for the future.
